Frontend developers are skilled in a variety of technologies, including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which are the fundamental building blocks of the web. They leverage these technologies to create responsive layouts, style elements, and implement client-side interactivity, ensuring that websites work seamlessly across different browsers and devices. In addition to technical prowess, frontend developers often collaborate closely with designers and backend developers to bring a cohesive digital vision to life.
